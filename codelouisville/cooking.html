Salmon Patties

Ingredients

  • 1 can salmon
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 can diced tomatoes drained
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • vegetables and dressing of choice

Instructions

  • Mix salmon, egg, and tomatos
  • add breadcrumbs,garlic, salt and pepper
  • mix well and then let sit for 15 min in fridge
  • heat a skillet with butter
  • make pattys and cover in breadcrumbs
  • Cook in skillet till salmon is completely cooked.

Stuffed Eggplant

Ingredients

  • eggplant
  • 1/2lb ground turkey
  • 1 can crab meat
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  • Core eggplant
  • mix turkey, crabmeat, egg, and cheese together
  • add seasonings.
  • preheat oven to 350. Fill eggplants with stuffing
  • Place in oven for 20 minutes
  • Remove from oven and put mozzarella on top of eggplant
  • Put back in oven for another 15 minutes.

Turkey Artichoke Pasta

Ingredients

  • sliced turkey
  • 1 artichoke
  • 1 brick cream cheese
  • pasta noodles
  • salt and pepper and garlic
  • 1/2cup olive oil

Instructions

  • mix artichoke hearts with creamcheese and seasonings
  • roll out turkey slices and place spoonful of stuffing inside
  • roll up the turkey and cook in a skillet till brown
  • boil pasta till al dente'
  • when pasta is finished put olive oil over pasta
  • place stuffed turkey on top of pasta
  • Enjoy!
